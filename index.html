# Hello Goat!

Welcome to Goat's developer documentation! Here you will find all of the resources associated with the usage, maintenance, and creation of our digital products. In the sidebar you will find tutorials and guides, or even general information on how we operate from a development standpoint.

# How can you contribute?

All of these docs are maintained from the Github repository here. For new pages or current changes, create a new branch and pull request once it's ready for review. After one of our staff has reviewed the changes, it'll be merged into the master branch (which will update this site concurrently).
